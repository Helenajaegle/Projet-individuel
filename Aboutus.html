About us


Everything you need to know about this project

This website has been realized within the framework of an individual school project in MMI Métiers du Multimédia et de l'Internet. We had a subject: "From amazons to "swing your pig,'' the faces of feminism", from that we were free to go to any more precise subject. I chose to talk about feminism in Egypt. Because I think that we don't talk about it much in African countries. I did mention some international facts to complete my site. I proposed a neutral vision and talked about the history of 3 women and their visions of feminism. Through this project I hope to meet the expectations of users who are questioning feminism.

Project carried out as a part of a pedagogical exercise in the department MMi of Montbéliard.
