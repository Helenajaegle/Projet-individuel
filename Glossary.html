Glossary

Activist

one who wants to act, without going through the back door. Someone who wants to act now.

Conditioning

influencing something or someone to give them certain characteristics.

Discrimination

action of generating differences towards a third party because of his difference.

Feminism

process aiming at posing equality on the different sexes, demonstrating the importance of the place of the woman in the society.

Intrepid

who does not back down in the face of adversity, who is not afraid of danger.

Militant

who fights / who rebels to make her ideas, opinions heard.

Patriarchal

when the man has the upper hand, the father must represent authority.

Stereotype

action / fact that represents a cliché

Subjection

the state of being dominated by something or someone, subject to the decisions of someone.
