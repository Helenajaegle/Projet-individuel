Timeline

Women from those past articles have all lived, fight for reduce injustices between women and men. Many key dates brought improvments. Here are important dates for women in Egypt.

1919

Women's protest against the British occupier

1923

International fame of Huda Sharawi for refusing to wear the veil

1943

The women's democratic league of Beirut met

1944

Demonstration in Damascus to demand the unveiling of the Muslim woman

1946

Organization of a general assembly of women's clubs in the Near East

1951

Demonstration in front of the parliament to obtain the right to vote

1979

Law 44 stipulates that husbands must confirm their social status when contracting another marriage

2000

Law regulating divorce, law concerning child custody rights

JANUARY 2011

Women account for 23% of parliamentarians, 20-25% of them are professionally active

2011

Egyptian women take to the streets to celebrate International Women's Day
